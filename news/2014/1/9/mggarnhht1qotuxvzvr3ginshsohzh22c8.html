EFFEKT is prequalified for Gellerup urban spaces competition

January 9, 2014 Mikkel Bogh

EFFEKT is pre-qualified for the competition for the new parks and urban spaces in Gellerup and Toveshøj - an ambitious and challenging project that requires a social inclusive approach and new recreational visions for an otherwise congested neighborhood in Aarhus. The task is to come up with suggestions on how the diverse urban spaces in Gellerup can be merged into a harmonious, prosperous and attractive neighborhood not only for the local residents but also for the remaining citizens of Aarhus. The team also consist of SLA, COWI, Social Action, Simon Prahm (GAM3) og Boris Brorman Jensen.
