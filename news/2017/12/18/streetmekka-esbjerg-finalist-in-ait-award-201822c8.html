Streetmekka Esbjerg finalist in AIT Award 2018

December 18, 2017 Lucrezia Biasutti

The finalists for 'AIT-Award 2018 l Best in Interior and Architecture' was announced last week, and our project Streetmekka Esbjerg is among the six finalists in the Sports+Leisure category. A total of 723 projects from 36 countries had been submitted for the award this year. In a closed session on 7th and 8th December 2017 in Hamburg, an appointed jury selected the best projects in each category. The winners will be announced on March 21st at an award ceremony in Frankfurt.
