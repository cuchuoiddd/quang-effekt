ReGen Villages on national Danish TV

June 14, 2016 Lucrezia Biasutti

This morning, EFFEKT's partner Sinus Lynge was interviewed on national Danish TV, talking about ReGen Villages. The interview was part of TV2's morning program Go'morgen Danmark.

Watch the interview online here.

We continue to receive hundreds of inquiries from both media, developers, land owners, governments and municipalities, as well as families wanting to live in ReGen Villages. The interest comes from all over the world, from Myanmar to Namibia, Iceland to Japan, U.S. to Peru and South Africa. A quick overview counts over 6,200 direct e-mails of interest within the past two weeks, nearly 200 developers and landowners wanting to partner with ReGen Villages, 14 national governments and 37 municipalities.
