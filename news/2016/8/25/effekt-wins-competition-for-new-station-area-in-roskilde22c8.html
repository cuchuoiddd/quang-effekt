EFFEKT wins competition for new station area in Roskilde

August 30, 2016 Lucrezia Biasutti

Today the result of the competition for the new Roskilde Station Area has been announced and the winning team led by EFFEKT includes karres+brands, WTM Engineers, ARUP and ALECTIA. The project reunites the city and reinvents the station as an integral part of the city centre, by transforming industrial waste land along the railway into a vibrant urban district and infrastructural hub.
