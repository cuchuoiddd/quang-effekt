EFFEKT part of new DAC exhibition at BLOX

May 2, 2018 Lucrezia Biasutti

Monday May 7th, the Danish Architecture Center (DAC) will open in new surroundings as part of the BLOX building on Copenhagen's harbour front. The opening exhibition 'Welcome Home' focuses on the future of Danish housing, and will feature no less than 5 EFFEKT projects - the transformation of Rosenhøj, the ecovillage design ReGen Villages, the vision for Ringkøbing K, and the masterplan for Gellerup, as well as the later landscape plan for Gellerup Toveshøj. The exhibition will run from May 7th to September 23rd 2018. As part of a special opening ceremony on May 5th, EFFEKT's partner Sinus Lynge will take part in a workshop and debate about how the homes of the future can embrace community and sustainability, in dialogue with architects from GXN, Henning Larsen and C.F. Møller.
