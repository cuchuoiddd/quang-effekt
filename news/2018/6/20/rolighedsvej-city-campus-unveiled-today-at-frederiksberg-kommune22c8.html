Rolighedsvej City Campus unveiled today at Frederiksberg Kommune

June 20, 2018 Lucrezia Biasutti

Our design proposal for Rolighedsvej City Campus is unveiled today at Frederiksberg City Hall with an opening ceremony to the exhibition of all the projects.

The area between Rolighedsvej and Bülowsvej is being developed to strengthen Frederiksberg´s vocation as a knowledge center. The vision is to create a vibrant City Campus that will host different functions including schools, student housing, residential units as well as workspaces and sports facilities.

The exhibition is open to the public and will display EFFEKT's proposal developed in collaboration with CFBO as well as the proposals of COBE and Entasis.

The competition is organized by Frederiksberg Kommune with NREP and Freja Ejendomme.
