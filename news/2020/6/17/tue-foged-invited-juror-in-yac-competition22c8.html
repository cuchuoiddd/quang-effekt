Tue Foged invited juror in YAC competition

May 5, 2020 Lucrezia Biasutti

EFFEKT co-founder Tue Foged is one of the jurors selected for the Tree House Module competition, organized by YAC – Young Architects Competitions and Dartagnans.

The ideas competitions will award a total of 5 prizes and 10 honorable mentions to the best designs for a treehouse system to sit amidst the oneiric French castles of Vibrac, Mothe Chandeniers, and Ebaupinay. The winner will also have the chance to realize the design.

Students as well as architects, designers, and artists are invited to participate.

Calendar:

26/04/2020 “early bird” registration ends

24/05/2020 “standard” registration ends

21/06/2020 “late” registration ends

24/06/2020 material submission deadline

More information at: www.youngarchitectscompetitions.com
