Last days of public vote for 2020 AZ Azure Awards

June 17, 2020 Lucrezia Biasutti

There is time until June 19th for the public to vote their favourite projects in the People's Choice Awards on the AZURE Awards website.

Both Camp Adventure Forest Tower and the Urban Village Project have been selected as finalists for the AZURE´s 2020 AZ Awards, in the categories Landscape Architecture and Concepts: Ideas/Prototypes respectively.

The two projects are among 68 finalists chosen by the international jury among 1241 entries from 47 countries.

Winners of both the People's Choice and those selected by the jury will be announced at the AZ Awards Gala in Toronto on Friday, September 18, 2020.
