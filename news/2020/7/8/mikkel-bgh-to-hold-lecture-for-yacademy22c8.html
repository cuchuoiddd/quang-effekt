Mikkel Bøgh to hold lecture for YACademy

July 8, 2020 Lucrezia Biasutti

This winter, EFFEKT´s Mikkel Bøgh will hold a special lecture for YACademy “Architecture for Landscape” course, presenting the case study of Camp Adventure Forest Tower as well as other EFFEKT projects that focus on reconnecting urban dwellers with nature.

With the aim of training designers to meet the diverse needs of transforming territories, the course encourages an attentive and productive dialogue with the surrounding landscape to respond to the needs of clients´unique contexts.
