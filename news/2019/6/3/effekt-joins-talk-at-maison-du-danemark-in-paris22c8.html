EFFEKT joins talk at Maison du Danemark in Paris

June 3, 2019 Lucrezia Biasutti

On Monday, June 3rd at the Maison du Danemark in Paris, EFFEKT's Head of Planning and Urban Projects Sonja Stockmarr will join Søren Øllgaard from Henning Larsen and Michele Zaoui from the Maire de Paris in a conversation about how to reinvent, restructure and optimize the urban infrastructure in order to improve the quality of life and address the contemporary climate challenges, with examples from the French and Danish approach.

The talk, moderated by journalist Louise Sandager, will start at 18.30 and will be conducted in English with a simultaneous translation in French.
