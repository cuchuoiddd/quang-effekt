GAME Streetmekka Viborg on the cover of sb magazine

November 20, 2019 Lucrezia Biasutti

GAME Streetmekka Viborg is on the cover of the issue 5/2019 of sb magazine, the international magazine for sports, leisure and recreational facilities.

The issue contains a thorough reports of the 2019 IOC IAKS Architecture Prizes, the awards granted by the International Olympic Committee. The Golden Medal was assigned to GAME Streetmekka Viborg in the 2019 edition, during the Award Gala held in Cologne, Germany on November 5th, 2019.
